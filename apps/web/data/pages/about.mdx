## Hello

I'm Trung, a software engineer who is fascinated by new technologies and constantly exploring better ways to build products.

My main stacks are **Java Spring Boot** and **React.js**. I also work with **Node.js**, **Angular**, **Next.js**, and **React Native** across product delivery, internal platforms, and workflow automation.

## Engineering mindset

I prefer pragmatic architecture: clear boundaries, simple contracts, observable behavior, and enough structure to let a product grow without slowing the team down.

That usually means starting with a maintainable default, measuring real bottlenecks, and introducing complexity only when the system actually needs it.

## What I care about

I enjoy the intersection between product delivery and engineering quality: shaping modules, keeping interfaces understandable, making systems easier to operate, and turning rough ideas into software that teams can confidently evolve.

Writing is part of that practice. It helps me clarify trade-offs, document decisions, and share patterns that are useful in real projects rather than only in idealized examples.
